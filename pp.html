Permission used in Tomato Safety are explained below.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is required to get the location of the device when the SIM card is changed. READ_PHONE_STATE is required to save the SIM card details in order to detect a SIM change scenario. SEND_SMS is required to send an SMS from the device when SIM card is changed and to retrieve the forgotten password.
